Projects

Volunteer

Pyvec doesn't give orders. Pyvec helps to those who want to achieve something — it's a nonprofit service organization. Starting a meetup in your town? Course in your school? Workshop? Screencast? Let's come up with a way to make it happen! E-mail us at info@pyvec.org.

  • PyCon CZ conference

    cz.pycon.org

    The largest yearly gathering of people interested in Python in the Czech Republic. Part of the international PyCon series.

    How Pyvec helps: Reimbursing equipment, accounting, contracts, legal service

  • Learn Python

    naucse.python.cz

    Open and freely accessible materials for beginner or advanced courses of the Python language. In Czech. Continuously developing and growing.

    How Pyvec helps: Operations

  • PyLadies CZ

    pyladies.cz

    International activity supporting women to get into tech with the help of the Python language. Czech groups hold semester-long free-of-charge beginner courses.

    How Pyvec helps: Reimbursing equipment, contracts, interconnection of local groups

  • Pyvo meetups

    pyvo.cz

    Informal developer meetups focused on talks on advanced topics, networking, or education. Organized monthly.

    How Pyvec helps: Reimbursing equipment, interconnection of local groups

  • PyWorking

    pyworking.cz

    Hands-on meetups for intermediate Python developers where anyone can come and consult their solutions or try out anything they like. In Prague these are complemented by occasional day-long workshops.

    How Pyvec helps: Reimbursing equipment, contracts

  • PyData Prague

    pydata.cz

    Irregular meetups with talks for lovers of data, Python and other languages, part of the PyData international educational programme.

    How Pyvec helps: Contracts

  • python.cz

    python.cz

    The main portal and source of information of the Czech Python user group.

    How Pyvec helps: Operations

  • Community blog

    blog.python.cz

    Czech Python user group news. Anyone can contribute with their article.

    How Pyvec helps: Operations

  • Community docs

    docs.pyvec.org

    A documentation of community operations, which also sums up know-how for organizers of user group events, such as meetups, workshops, courses, etc.

    How Pyvec helps: Operations

Long-term supporters

Supporter

Thanks to our efforts, there are more programmers on the Czech market. Financial support of Pyvec is your investment in education, healthier job market and the growth of the Python community. Use our transparent bank account, Benevity, or e-mail us at info@pyvec.org.

Who is Pyvec

Organization

Pyvec is a nonprofit founded in spring 2012. The board takes care of financial matters and makes decisions on other important issues. Become a member and influence how Pyvec supports the Czech Python user group!
